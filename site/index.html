A plan-driven toolkit for Claude Code — so the work finishes when you do.

The problem

AI-assisted development is a superpower. It's also a radical transformation. The creative vision flows faster than ever. You can build things you couldn't before.

But without guardrails, the tools make decisions you didn't ask for — and you're left picking up the pieces.

AI doesn't reduce work — it intensifies it. — Harvard Business Review, 2026

What plankit does

Plans that direct Claude toward predictable, correct results. Guidelines that prevent the nightmare sessions and keep Claude from rediscovering your project every conversation. Skills that turn your workflow into slash commands.

Each layer compounds on the last. Claude operates with confidence; you stay in control of direction.

  • Plan Review the plan, not the code. Claude proposes, you direct. Once approved, plans are preserved to docs/plans/ — timestamped, committed, and protected from edits. The reasoning outlives the session.
  • Guard Branch protection that runs locally, before the damage happens. Rules that prevent the common mistakes: force pushes, silent failures, scope creep, shortcuts without permission, untested changes.
  • Ship Changelog generated from conventional commits. Git tags as the single source of truth. One command to tag, one to push — commit and push stay separate decisions. Pre-flight checks before anything leaves your machine.
